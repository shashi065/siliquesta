for Perfect Design.

SILIQUESTA is the world's first AI-native EDA platform combining real CMOS physics, Claude AI intelligence, autonomous design agents, and digital twin reliability — running entirely in your browser.

⚡
Real Physics Engine
Validated CMOS equations. t_pd = C_L·VDD/I_d. Every click produces real silicon-physics computation — not approximations or mocked data.
🧠
Claude AI Integration
Full Claude 3.5 Sonnet with smart offline fallback. Analyze designs, generate RTL/SPICE, predict failures — zero API errors shown to user.
🎯
ADA — Autonomous Agent
Sweeps 500,000+ design points per session. Multi-objective Pareto optimization. Finds designs you'd never find manually.
🔮
Digital Twin
ML-based reliability model with NBTI, HCI, and electromigration aging. Predict chip health over 25-year lifetime. Self-healing AVS policies.
🌡
Full PVT Analysis
All 6 process corners (SS/TT/FF/SF/FS/MC), -40°C to 125°C sweep, 0.5–3.3V supply range. Monte Carlo statistical analysis.
🔍
Explainable AI (XAI)
SHAP-based feature importance. Understand exactly why the AI made each recommendation. Transparent, auditable decisions.
💾
Design DNA
Vector-embedded design memory. Every circuit you design becomes training data for your private AI model. Your moat compounds.
📱
Cross-Platform PWA
Install on Android, iOS, Windows, macOS from browser. Full offline operation. Works everywhere without Docker or setup.
⚛
Angstrom-Era Ready
Quantum tunneling predictor, 2D materials, CFET models. Built for 1nm and beyond where traditional EDA tools break down.
